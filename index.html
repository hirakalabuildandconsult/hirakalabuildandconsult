Who We Are

Hirakala Build and Consult is a multidisciplinary engineering and consultancy company providing professional services in civil engineering, architecture, project management, surveying, estimation, BIM, construction supervision and infrastructure development.

We strive to deliver innovative, sustainable and cost-effective solutions for residential, commercial and infrastructure projects.

What We Do

Architectural Design

Modern and sustainable building design solutions.

Structural Design

Building Information Modeling (BIM)

3D modeling, coordination and clash detection services.

Construction Supervision
